    Hours: Monday - Friday, 6am - 5pm PST

  • Report an Adverse Event

    Report a side effect or harmful experience while taking a Genentech medicine.
    Phone: (888) 835-2555
    Hours: 24 hrs/day, 7 days/week

    Mail: Genentech US Drug Safety
    1 DNA Way Mailstop 258A
    South San Francisco, CA 94080

    Fax: (650) 225-4630
